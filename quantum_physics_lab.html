Particle and Quantum Physics Lab

This lab is a simulation of the behaviors of fundamental particles.
It specifically focuses on the properties of different types of particles,
such as mass, charge, spin and colour charge.

The interactions of this simulation are not completely accurate.
It is designed to teach properties of particles and interactions,
but wave-like behaviors e.g. wavefunctions and probability distributions are not included.
